# FMC Technologies / TechnipFMC (fmc-technologies)

FMC Technologies was a Houston-based energy services and equipment company serving the upstream oil and gas industry, with particular focus on subsea, surface, and measurement solutions for hydrocarbon production. In January 2017 FMC Technologies completed its merger with Technip to form TechnipFMC, a global energy services company serving the subsea, surface, and offshore project markets across roughly 48 countries.

This repository tracks the legacy FMC Technologies brand and any successor APIs, developer tools, and machine-readable artifacts associated with the merged TechnipFMC entity. As of this revision no public APIs or developer programs have been documented.
